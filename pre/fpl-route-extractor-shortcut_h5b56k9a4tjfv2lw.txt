## Extract and copy waypoint identifiers as a space-delimited list.

This shortcut extracts *exclusively* the usable identifiers from `.fpl` (flight plan) files and copies them as a space-delimited list. I originally created it to quickly copy [*Infinite Flight*](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/infinite-flight-simulator/id471341991) flight plan information from the share sheet. 

Example output:

```
KMIA KROCS RUSSS MRENO BNGOS COREA DOLIE SMELZ JAWJA GOOGY WASER ERASE CRAMM VANZE CHLMR HADAN MCGHE RW36R KMEM
```
